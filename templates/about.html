About HealthYou

Welcome to HealthYou, your trusted companion for maintaining daily health routines. Our app is designed to help you stay on track with your health goals through personalized reminders and easy-to-use features.

This project was inspired by my personal journey to maintain a healthier lifestyle amidst a busy schedule. As someone who often forgot to drink water, take vitamins, or even stretch during long coding sessions, I realized the need for a simple, effective tool to help me stay on track with my health routines. HealthYou was born out of this necessity and evolved into a comprehensive health management app. This is a Portfolio Project for Holberton School, showcasing the skills and knowledge I've gained. You can learn more about the program here.

Our Mission

At HealthYou, our mission is to make healthy living simple and achievable for everyone. We believe that small, consistent actions lead to significant improvements in overall health and well-being.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is HealthYou?

HealthYou is an app designed to help you maintain your daily health routines by providing personalized reminders and tips.

How can I set up a reminder?

You can set up a reminder by navigating to the "Set Your Reminders" section, selecting the type of reminder and interval, and clicking the "Set Reminder" button.

Is HealthYou free to use?

Yes, HealthYou is free to use. We believe in making health accessible to everyone.
